Our recommendation

WAGNER TUNING - Your expert for high-performance intercoolers and high-quality engine parts from Dessau-Roßlau / Germany

The intercooler is undoubtedly one of the essential components of any car with a supercharged engine. This supercharging is effectively supported by the intercooler, which cools down the airflow leaving the compressor significantly. The team at WAGNER TUNING has specialized in the development, design and production of intercoolers and other engine parts such as exhaust manifolds and Y-pipes - also for racing. Thanks to innovative development software and measurement technology, highest precision in production and high quality demands on ourselves and our products, we have become a globally operating company with a constantly growing circle of satisfied customers. Around 3000 dealers now work successfully with us in almost every country in the world. Despite our success, we remain true to our roots: The headquarters of WAGNER TUNING have been located in Dessau-Roßlau since its foundation in 2002.

Special intercoolers for special vehicles

WAGNER TUNING offers its customers a wide range of intercoolers and engine parts for a variety of vehicle manufacturers and models. These are clearly listed in the online shop according to manufacturer name, so that it is clear at first glance which parts are available for the respective models. Your vehicle is something very special and the intercooler should be just as special? Then WAGNER TUNING is the right partner for you: In addition to our large selection in the shop, you have the possibility to use our intercooler configurator to create your own intercooler perfectly matched to the respective application in just a few steps and to have this special design produced by us. Our experienced employees in the production department accept every challenge.

Experience, quality and innovation - combined at the highest level

For many years, the WAGNER TUNING team has been working with high motivation and passion in the development and production of high-quality engine parts and intercoolers. Our customers appreciate our many years of experience and our high quality work in the field of engine optimization and the associated performance increase. At WAGNER TUNING, there is no standing still - our development engineers are always on the pulse of the latest developments and our workplaces are equipped with the latest measuring technology, software and tools. The high quality standards that we set for our products and ourselves are laid down in our in-house quality management system and have been verified without complaint by an officially recognised monitoring organisation since 2013. The KBA verification guidelines applied correspond to a quality management standard DIN EN ISO 9002.

Short processing time and competent advice

At WAGNER TUNING, we do not only give our all when it comes to engine optimisation: we work precisely and efficiently when it comes to processing your order or a product enquiry. In our warehouse in Dessau-Roßlau we have a large selection of intercooler kits of almost all common manufacturers permanently in stock on a storage area of about 10,000 sqm. Thanks to the most modern shipping logistics we can quickly get them on their way to you. Before you place an order, do you have a technical question or would you like advice on engine optimisation and performance enhancement? You are interested in Wagner Clothing? Let us talk about it! You can reach us by telephone during our business hours or send us a message using our contact form. The team of WAGNER TUNING is looking forward to your request!
